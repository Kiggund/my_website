About Us

We, the members of Youth Connect Group, recognize the importance of empowering youth as a cornerstone for sustainable development. United by a shared vision to promote financial literacy, encourage saving, and create investment opportunities, we work collaboratively to transform challenges into opportunities for young people. With a deep sense of responsibility, integrity, and inclusiveness, we strive for lasting impact in the community.

Historical Background

Youth Connect Group was established in early 2021 with a mission to empower youth through financial literacy, saving programs, and investment management. Founded by Wawumba Esuneri, the group initially operated under a different name but rebranded to reflect a modern and relatable identity. The name "Youth Connect Group", proposed by Kawala Josephyne Fridah, symbolizes the group's mission to connect young people with opportunities for personal and community development.

Mission and Vision

Mission: To promote financial savings and investment management among the youth for collective economic empowerment.

Vision: To create a unified, sustainable future by investing in businesses with strong leadership and empowering youth through collaboration and strategic planning.
